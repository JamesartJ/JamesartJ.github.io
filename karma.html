About the Project

Prior to the presence of both Uber and Lyft in Portland, students at Lewis & Clark College set out to create a volunteer ride service. By virtue of their attendance, each student has an lclark ID. This account information is then used for both requesting rides as well as tracking the driving of other students. Volunteering to drive other students earns "Karma points" which the app tracks. These points can then be spent on future rides, or on special rewards offered by the college. After login, the landing screen allows users to request rides. If the user wishes to earn points by giving rides, they must navigate to the points section.
