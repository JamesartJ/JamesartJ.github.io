Stockt

About the Project

Stockt, formerly known as Pindi, is a mobile-first marketplace for restaurants, allowing restaurant owners to source from multiple vendors in one unified storefront. As the projects product designer I performed user research and designed the information architecture, user experience, interaction and interface.

Shopping

In order for users to balance their minimum order requirements from distributors, users shop amongst different vendor storefronts. Each vendor page includes additional information about their product offerings along with their delivery schedule.

Searching

Users can search at many different levels throughout the platform, top-level among vendors, within a vendor’s offerings, and also among product categories. Results for top-level searches are displayed partitioned by vendor, allowing users to compare offerings.

The option for intelligent cart optimization was not within the project’s scope. So products in the cart are again partitioned by vendor to ensure minimum order requirements are easily managed.
