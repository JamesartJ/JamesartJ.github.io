My Work

Ebay

I worked as the lead UX designer on app for eBay's charity department in collaboration with the nonprofit Oregon Gear Up. I performed the user research and designed both the app's interaction and visual treatment.

abroad

I designed Abroad's initial branding, and designed the app's UX. As their slogan "live like a local abroad" suggests, Abroad is an iOS app that connects users to local events while studying in foreign countries.

venu

I worked for Venu as a brand designer, and later was hired to design the patform's UX. Venu is a web platform which allows users to collaborate with local artists in order to host events.

trngle

I worked with Trngle's founders to design the app's UX and and interface. Trngle is an iOS app that aims to network together creatives and allows them to collaborate on projects.

Artists Teaching Art

I worked with the Sausalito Art Festival to design brand and website for Artists Teaching Art. Artists Teaching Art program is a nonprofit which supports the arts in Marin County schools.

Domenico Vacca

This redesign looked to update Domenico Vacca's online identity as well as their web store. This was a mockup presented to the design firm hired to update their web presence.
