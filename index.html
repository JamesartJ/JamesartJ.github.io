UX, UI and Brand Designer currently based in Portland.

venu

Venu is a web platform designed to allow users to collaborate with artists in order to host events. The platform connects local artists with spaces to showcase their work, whether in a concert hall or someone's backyard. I worked with the founder of Venu, Andrew Ellen, to brand and design the patform itself.

abroad app

As their slogan "live like a local abroad" suggests, the Abroad App is an iOS app that connects users to local events while studying in foreign countries. We worked with the app's creator, Matt Sanders, to design the company's branding and the UI and UX elements for the app itself.

trngle app

Trngle is an iOS app that aims to network together creatives and allows them to collaborate on projects. Through Trngle these teams can also broadcast their work to the world. I worked with the founders to design the app's UX and UI and also designed the app's interface.

Artists Teaching Art

The Sausalito Art Festival Foundation's Artists Teaching Art program is a nonprofit which supports the arts in Marin County school systems. I worked with the owners of the Sausalito Art Festival, as well as the heads of Artists Teaching Art to design and develop their brand identity, logo, and website.

Domenico Vacca

This redesign looked to update Domenico Vacca's online identity as well as its boutique, while maintaining the brand's classic image of excellence. This was a mockup presented to the design firm contracted by Domenico Vacca to update their web presence.
