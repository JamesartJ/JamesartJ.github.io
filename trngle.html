Trngle App

To create a logo that embodied the Abroad brand, we looked into the purpose and meaning of the app. Abroad aims to unite students studying abroad and ultimately make them feel more comfortable and informed in foreign landscapes. As the tagline: "live like a local abroad" suggests, Abroad allows students to share local events and places with others in their community. The app connects people and ensures they are in the loop. We then distilled these ideas into three distinct symbols.
